Welcome to Inaindha Kaigal

Inaindha Kaigal is a participative drive, where likeminded people come together to help an under-privileged child reach his / her full potential through education. If you are someone who wants to make a difference in the life of a child, you can do that by contributing your time for our various initiatives. Inaindha Kaigal is an organisation that is completely run by volunteers. Volunteers are the pillars of strength for Inaindha Kaigal and they play a key role in every part of Inaindha Kaigal’s initiatives. Many of our projects would not be successful without their continuing support and contribution.

Inaindha Kaigal also provides individualistic attention to every student through the mentorship program, by connecting every student with a mentor, who is not a parent or a teacher but someone who believes in the importance of education and the student’s ability to achieve. You can become a Mentor, if you have the inclination and the time.
